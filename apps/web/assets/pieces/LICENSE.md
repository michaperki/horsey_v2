# Chess Piece Assets

Active board pieces are generated from individual SVG files published on OpenGameArt:

- Source: https://opengameart.org/content/chess-pieces-in-svg-format
- Author: femrek
- License: CC0

Generated files:

- `wK.svg`, `wQ.svg`, `wR.svg`, `wB.svg`, `wN.svg`, `wP.svg`
- `bK.svg`, `bQ.svg`, `bR.svg`, `bB.svg`, `bN.svg`, `bP.svg`

The generated files recolor the single-color originals for Horsey's light and dark sides.
